About Us

Signs by Renée opened in Marathon in 1977. We are a traditional full service shop, offering quality workmanship and service from the first steps of design to the completed project. Our goal for you is to create striking signage that will benefit your business. We will provide you with the best options and ideas to produce your project based on where and how your signs will be used and what image you want your business to project. We will make recommendations on colors, type styles, and materials for your signs. Our shop uses only the best materials available. That, along with the latest equipment, and combined with our experience, enables us to produce a product that will help your business get the attention it deserves. Signs by Renée is a licensed sign contractor. We can take you through the complete permitting process to the final sign installation.
